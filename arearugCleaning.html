OUR AREA RUG CLEANING PROCESS

Step 1: Inspection -

Our cleaning expert will go around your home with you to look closely at the rug. They'll identify any tough stains and tell you what results to expect.

Step 2: Vacuum -

We start by using a good vacuum to get rid of dry dirt. Taking away dry dirt is a really important part of the cleaning process

Step 3: Pre-Spray -

We use a special enzyme spray to break down dirt in high-traffic areas and common spots, ensuring a more thorough cleaning.

Step 4: Pre-Spot -

We use special solutions on tough spots before cleaning to improve the chances of getting rid of them..

Step 5: Extraction and Rinse -

Next, we make sure to thoroughly rinse the area rug using an extremely powerful hot water extraction cleaning system. We take care not to overly wet the carpet or leave any sticky residue behind.

Step 7: Post Cleaning Inspection -

Our technician will go around your home with you to show you the cleaning results and make sure you are completely satisfied with the job.
