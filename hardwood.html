REMOVE WAX BUILDUP AND REVIVE YOUR WOOD FLOORS

If you're looking to get rid of wax buildup and bring back the natural beauty of your hardwood floors, our wood floor wax removal service is ideal. Over time, wax, oil, and acrylic-based cleaners from the store can make your floors look darker and less appealing. These products often leave a heavy residue that attracts dirt, grime, and bacteria, leading to a darkened appearance and making your floors more prone to scratches and stains.

At Capital Carpet Solutions, we provide an effective solution to this issue. Our service not only removes the wax but also gives your floors a deep clean. For floors with years of accumulated wax and residue, we recommend following up with a screen and recoat service. The choice of cleaning method for your upholstery depends on the specific fabric. Drying time is influenced by both the cleaning method and the optional application of a protector, with typical dry times ranging from 2-6 hours.

Cleaning upholstery can differ significantly depending on the fabric. Before cleaning, make sure to check the manufacturer's cleaning instructions. Typically, the tag on upholstered furniture will have one of four different letters

• W-You can clean this material using water.
• S-No water. You'll need to purchase a special solvent-based cleaner.
• WS-You can use water or a solvent-based cleaner on this sofa.
• X-Vacuum only.

This information will guide you in selecting the appropriate cleaning method for your upholstery.
